BIOGRAPHY

Makoto Shinkai is a renowned Japanese animated filmmaker known for his visually stunning and emotionally evocative films. Born on February 9, 1973, in Nagano, Japan, Shinkai gained international recognition for his breakthrough film "Your Name" (2016), which became a global sensation and one of the highest-grossing anime films of all time. His works often explore themes of love, distance, and the profound impact of natural phenomena. With his unique storytelling style and breathtaking animation, Shinkai has captivated audiences worldwide. His films, including "The Garden of Words" (2013) and "Weathering with You" (2019), have received critical acclaim and numerous awards, solidifying his position as a visionary director in the world of animation.

Legacy and Influence

Makoto Shinkai's films have left an indelible mark on the world of animation. His unique blend of visually striking imagery, profound storytelling, and heartfelt emotions have captivated audiences worldwide. Shinkai's works have inspired countless artists and filmmakers, and his ability to explore the depths of human emotions continues to resonate with viewers of all ages.

ANIME & MOVIES

Weathering With You

Set during a period of exceptionally rainy weather, high-school boy Hodaka Morishima runs away from his troubled rural home to Tokyo and befriends an orphan girl who can manipulate the weather.

Your Name

Two teenagers share a profound, magical connection upon discovering they are swapping bodies. Things manage to become even more complicated when the boy and girl decide to meet in person.

Garden Of Words

When a lonely teenager decides to miss his morning lessons in favour of sitting in a lovely garden, he meets a mysterious older woman who shares his feelings of alienation.

5 Centimeters per Second

5 Centimeters per Second is a 2007 Japanese romantic drama animated film written and directed by Makoto Shinkai. It theatrically premiered on 3 March 2007. The film consists of three segments, each following a period in the protagonist Takaki Tōno's life and his relationships with the girls around him.

Suzume

As the skies turn red and the planet trembles, Japan stands on the brink of disaster. However, a determined teenager named Suzume sets out on a mission to save her country. Able to see supernatural forces that others cannot, it's up to her to close the mysterious doors that are spreading chaos across the land. A perilous journey awaits as the fate of Japan rests on her shoulders.

Flavors of Youth

Three stories of youth from different cities in China: memories locked in a bowl of steaming noodles, a fading beauty discovering her way, and a bittersweet first love.
